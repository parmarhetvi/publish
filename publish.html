Steps to Publish Your Website

1

Choose a Hosting Provider

Select a web hosting service that fits your needs. Options include shared hosting, VPS, cloud hosting, or specialized platforms like Netlify or Vercel for static sites.

2

Register a Domain Name

Choose and register a unique domain name for your website. Many hosting providers offer domain registration services, or you can use specialized registrars.

3

Prepare Your Website Files

Ensure all your HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and asset files are organized. Test your website locally to fix any issues before uploading.

4

Upload Your Files

Use FTP, SFTP, or your hosting provider's file manager to upload your website files to the server. Typically, you'll upload to the public_html or www directory.

5

Test Your Live Website

After uploading, thoroughly test your website using the live URL. Check all pages, links, forms, and functionality to ensure everything works correctly.
